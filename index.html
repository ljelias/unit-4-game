~ ~ ~  Gem Collector  ~ ~ ~

At the start of the game, you will see a random number. Try to match that number by clicking on the gems.

The gems have hidden values. You will discover the values by clicking on them.

Each time you click on a gem, the value of that gem will be added to your total score.

You win by matching the random number. If you go above that number, you lose.

Each time the game starts, the gems will change their value.
