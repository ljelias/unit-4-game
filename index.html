Gem Collector Game

OBJECTIVE: Make your total match the random number.

  • Each gem has a random value.
  • Clicking on a gem adds that gem's value to your total.
  • If you match the random number, you win.
  • If you go above the random number, you lose.
  • After you win or lose, the game restarts, and each gem has a new random value.
